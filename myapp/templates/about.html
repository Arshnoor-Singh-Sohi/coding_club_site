About the University Coding Club

Founded in 2018 by a group of enthusiastic students, the University Coding Club has grown into a vibrant community of coders, developers, and tech-enthusiasts. Our mission is to foster collaboration, skill sharing, and innovation through various events and activities.

Over the years, we've hosted numerous workshops, hackathons, hack-nights, and career panels. Our alumni have gone on to work at top tech companies, startups, and research labs. The club is open to all majorsâ€”if you have an interest in coding, there's a place for you here.

Our Milestones

  1. September 2018: Club founded by five CS majors.
  2. January 2019: First hackathon with 50 participants.
  3. April 2020: Introduced virtual workshops during pandemic.
  4. June 2021: Launched mentorship program pairing seniors with freshmen.
  5. October 2022: Organized county-wide coding competition.
  6. February 2024: Partnered with industry sponsors for internship pipelines.
